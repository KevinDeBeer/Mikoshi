# Privacy Policy

**Last updated:** March 15, 2026

## 1. Introduction

This application ("the App") is a private content publishing tool used by its developer to manage and publish content to social media platforms such as Facebook and Instagram.

The App is not intended for public use and does not provide services to external users.

This Privacy Policy explains how information may be accessed and used when interacting with Meta (Facebook and Instagram) APIs.

---

## 2. Information Collected

The App may access certain information through the Meta platform APIs in order to publish content.

This may include:

* Facebook Page identifiers
* Instagram Business Account identifiers
* Access tokens provided by Meta
* Media container identifiers
* Media publishing status
* Basic account metadata required for content publishing

The App **does not collect personal information from external users**.

Since the App is used solely by its developer to manage their own pages and accounts, no user profiles, contact information, or personal data from third parties are collected.

---

## 3. How Information Is Used

Information accessed through Meta APIs is used exclusively for the following purposes:

* Publishing images and videos to Facebook and Instagram
* Scheduling social media posts
* Managing automated content publishing workflows
* Monitoring the status of published posts

The App does **not**:

* Sell personal data
* Share personal data with third parties
* Use personal data for advertising or analytics

---

## 4. Data Storage

Authentication credentials and access tokens required for interacting with Meta APIs may be stored securely by the developer to enable authorized API requests.

No personal user data from third parties is stored or processed by the App.

---

## 5. Data Sharing

The App does not share data with any third parties.

All communication occurs directly between the App and Meta platforms (Facebook and Instagram) through official APIs.

---

## 6. Data Retention

Any information accessed through Meta APIs is retained only as long as necessary to operate the App and publish content.

Because the App is a private internal tool, data retention is limited to operational requirements.

---

## 7. Security

Reasonable technical and organizational measures are implemented to protect stored access credentials and API tokens used to interact with Meta services.

---
